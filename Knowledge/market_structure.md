# Market Structure

## Bullish Structure

- Higher High (HH)
- Higher Low (HL)

## Bearish Structure

- Lower High (LH)
- Lower Low (LL)

## Break of Structure (BOS)

A BOS is valid only when:

- A significant swing is broken.
- The candle closes beyond the level.
- The move is supported by displacement.

## Change of Character (CHOCH)

CHOCH is valid when:

- Trend direction changes.
- Opposite structure begins forming.
- Supported by liquidity sweep.

## Rules

Ignore weak internal breaks.

Prefer external swing confirmation.

Always align execution with HTF structure.
