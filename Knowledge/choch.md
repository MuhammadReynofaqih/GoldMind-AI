# Change of Character (CHOCH)

## Definition

CHOCH represents the first confirmed indication that market structure may be changing.

Unlike BOS, CHOCH signals a possible reversal.

## Requirements

A valid CHOCH requires:

- Previous trend exhaustion.
- Liquidity sweep.
- Strong displacement.
- Candle close beyond the structural level.
- Higher timeframe confirmation.

## Invalid CHOCH

Ignore CHOCH when:

- It occurs inside consolidation.
- There is no displacement.
- It is immediately rejected.
- Macro conditions contradict the setup.

## Trading Rule

CHOCH alone is never enough.

Always combine with:

- Order Block
- Liquidity
- Fair Value Gap
- HTF Bias
