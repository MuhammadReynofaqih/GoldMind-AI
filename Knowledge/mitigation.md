# Mitigation Block

## Definition

A Mitigation Block is an institutional price area where large orders are revisited before continuation.

Institutions often return to mitigate previous positions before moving toward new liquidity.

## Characteristics

- Usually follows displacement.
- Often overlaps with Order Blocks.
- Frequently aligns with Fair Value Gaps.
- Higher probability during London or New York sessions.

## Trading Rule

Never trade a Mitigation Block without confirmation from market structure.
