# Break of Structure (BOS)

## Definition

Break of Structure (BOS) occurs when price decisively breaks a significant swing high or swing low in the direction of the prevailing trend.

A valid BOS confirms trend continuation rather than reversal.

## Requirements

A BOS is valid only if:

- A significant swing level is broken.
- The candle closes beyond the level.
- Strong displacement is present.
- The move is supported by increased volume when available.
- The break aligns with the higher timeframe trend.

## Invalid BOS

A BOS is considered invalid if:

- Only the wick breaks the level.
- No candle closes beyond the level.
- The move lacks displacement.
- The break occurs directly into strong opposing supply or demand.

## Trading Rule

Never trade solely because a BOS appears.

A valid BOS must be confirmed by:

- Liquidity Sweep
- Order Block
- Fair Value Gap
- Higher Timeframe Bias
- Risk Management
