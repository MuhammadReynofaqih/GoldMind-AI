# Liquidity

## Types

- Buy Side Liquidity
- Sell Side Liquidity
- Internal Liquidity
- External Liquidity

## Institutional Logic

Liquidity is the market's fuel.

Price seeks liquidity before expanding.

## Liquidity Sweep

A valid liquidity sweep:

- Removes stop-loss orders.
- Creates displacement.
- Leads to BOS or CHOCH.

## Rules

Do not trade solely because liquidity is swept.
