# Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

## Core Philosophy

The market is driven by institutional order flow.

Retail traders react.
Institutions create liquidity.

## Market Logic

Price moves from liquidity to liquidity.

Institutional objectives:

- Accumulate
- Manipulate
- Distribute

## Core Components

- Liquidity
- Market Structure
- BOS
- CHOCH
- Order Block
- Fair Value Gap
- Premium & Discount
- Inducement

## Rules

Never trade before liquidity is taken.

Never enter against HTF bias.

Require confirmation before execution.

Capital preservation is always the priority.
