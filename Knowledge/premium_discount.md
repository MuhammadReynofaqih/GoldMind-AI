# Premium & Discount

## Premium Zone

Price trades above equilibrium.

Institutional traders generally prefer selling in premium when bearish conditions exist.

## Discount Zone

Price trades below equilibrium.

Institutional traders generally prefer buying in discount when bullish conditions exist.

## Equilibrium

The 50% level of the dealing range.

Premium > 50%

Discount < 50%

## Trading Rule

Premium and Discount should always be evaluated together with:

- Market Structure
- Liquidity
- Order Blocks
- Fibonacci Retracement
