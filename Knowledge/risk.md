# Risk Rules

## Core Principles

Capital preservation is always the first priority.

Never risk more than 1% of total account equity on a single trade.

If the daily loss reaches 3%, stop trading for the day.

If the weekly loss reaches 6%, stop trading until the following week.

Never increase lot size to recover losses.

Never average down on losing positions.

Never move Stop Loss further away after entering a trade.

Always calculate lot size before entering a trade.

## Position Rules

Maximum one open position at a time.

Minimum Risk Reward Ratio: 1:2

Preferred Risk Reward Ratio: 1:3 or higher.

## No Trade Conditions

Do not trade if:

- Major news is within 30 minutes.
- HTF trend is unclear.
- Liquidity has not been taken.
- BOS or CHOCH is unconfirmed.
- Confidence score is below 70.
