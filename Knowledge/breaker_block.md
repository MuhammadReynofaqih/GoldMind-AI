# Breaker Block

## Definition

A Breaker Block is a failed Order Block that later becomes a support or resistance zone after market structure changes.

## Characteristics

- Appears after a valid BOS or CHOCH.
- Previously respected Order Block fails.
- Price later retests the failed zone.
- Institutional traders often use the retest for entry.

## Trading Rule

A Breaker Block has higher probability when aligned with:

- HTF Trend
- Liquidity Sweep
- Fair Value Gap
